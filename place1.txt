The Eiffel Tower is an iconic landmark in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 for the World's Fair. Gustave Eiffel designed this architectural wonder. Standing tall at 324 meters, it offers breathtaking views of Paris. Tourists from all over the world visit the Eiffel Tower. The structure lights up beautifully at night. There are three observation decks for visitors. The tower is made of iron and weighs approximately 10,000 tons. It took two years to build. Initially, many Parisians criticized its design. Over time, it became a symbol of love and culture. Couples often propose at the Eiffel Tower. The surrounding gardens are perfect for a picnic. There are restaurants inside the tower as well. During holidays, special light shows are organized. The tower has even hosted car races up its stairs. Over 7 million people visit it every year. The view of the River Seine from the top is stunning. It remains one of the most photographed landmarks worldwide. Visiting the Eiffel Tower is a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
